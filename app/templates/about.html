Our Story

Make Waves started in 2025 right in the middle of Temple Bar, Dublin. A group of local musicians — tired of overpriced studios and cold, clinical recording spaces — decided to build the kind of place they always wished existed.

What began as a passion project quickly turned into a real home for artists, bands, and creators looking for a space that feels more like a creative hangout than a studio on the clock. We’re musicians first, so we get it — the nerves, the magic, the need for the right vibe.

At Make Waves, we combine pro-level gear with a laid-back, welcoming atmosphere. Whether you’re cutting your first track, producing a full record, or just experimenting with ideas, we’re here to support the process every step of the way.

No egos, no pressure — just great sound, good people, and a space where your ideas can take shape.
